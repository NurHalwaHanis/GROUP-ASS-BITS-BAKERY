About Us


Tradition since 1999

Bakery bits is a malaysian bakery that was founded in 1999 by bakers addict, The Jemah's.

The idea for establishment of this bakery was a combination of opinion and efforts by the four head that we called The Jemah's Cantik. The inner and outer design of this bakery was designed by Sabrina and Halwa. Meanwhile for the production of dessert box, the idea was contributed by Ain and Syakilah. The obsession of us towards bakery has been the strength to keep this bakery alive from back then until now.

In 2002, we decided to expand this business towards a long term and famous bakery. Thus, this website and bakery bits teams was born. The bakery bits teams has managed to fuldfilled 10,000 online orders from 2002 until May 2021 and are blessed with the support from our beloved cutie customers.

We, from the Bakery Bits believes that everyone should taste the premium chocolate taste as chocolate is a theraphy for people. In fact, chocolate could bring happiness and the taste could makes you feels like live in world heaven!
